Inventory Management System

Leadership Review Presentation

This initiative will centralize asset and inventory operations, improve audit readiness, and provide leadership with real-time visibility into operations and compliance.

Executive Summary

The proposed system will replace fragmented manual processes with a secure, auditable, and scalable platform for managing assets, stock, workflows, audits, and reports. It will improve operational accountability and support faster, more confident decision-making at the leadership level.

Business Value
Higher traceability, stronger control, and faster audits
Primary Users
Administrators, Inventory Managers, Auditors, Department Heads
Core Outcome
One platform for asset and inventory visibility

Key Objectives

  • Centralize asset and inventory records
  • Improve stock accuracy and asset accountability
  • Accelerate audit planning and execution
  • Strengthen reporting and management oversight
  • Support role-based security and compliance

Scope Overview

In Scope

  • Asset registration and lifecycle
  • Inventory and stock movement
  • Audit management
  • Barcode and QR scanning
  • Dashboards and reports

Out of Scope

  • Full ERP financial accounting
  • Payroll and HR processing
  • Advanced AI forecasting

Persona Summary

Super Administrator

Manages users, roles, and system settings.

Inventory Manager

Tracks stock movement, vendors, and reorder levels.

Auditor

Conducts audits using mobile and batch scanning.

Department Head

Reviews department assets and maintenance status.

The BRD defines the following business capabilities that will be delivered through the platform.

Asset RegistrationRegister and identify every asset with a unique code and supporting metadata.Improves accountability and reduces lost or duplicate assets.
Asset Lifecycle TrackingTrack assignment, transfer, maintenance, repair, retirement, donation, and disposal.Provides full traceability from acquisition to end-of-life.
Inventory ManagementManage stock levels, stock-in/out movements, warehouses, shelves, and bins.Reduces discrepancies and improves stock visibility.
Organization Structure ManagementModel campuses, buildings, floors, rooms, departments, and cost centers.Supports structured reporting and ownership by unit.
Purchase & Receiving WorkflowsSupport request, approval, purchase order, and receiving activities.Connects operational activity to buying and receiving controls.

Barcode & QR ScanningSupport mobile and bulk scanning for fast identification of assets and stock.Improves speed and accuracy during field and audit activities.
Reporting & DashboardsShow trends, pending actions, low-stock conditions, and audit progress.Enables executive oversight and faster decisions.
User Management & SecurityProvide role-based access, audit logs, and controlled permissions.Protects data and enforces governance.
